Unique, intuitive and powerful framework for media playback.

Download

Any Device

Fabuloos is a radically faster, cleaner and simpler approach to set-up top-quality media players in a fraction of the time. Whether you're an expert developer or just getting started, it plays your videos perfectly on any browser and any device.

Unified API

Fabuloos offers a toolset for building the media player most suited to your site or app. The API is fully modular, works with your prefered hosted service (like YouTube), and gives you the ability to assemble exactly the player you need.

Full Experience

Tell Fabuloos stories and create web documentaries, slides alongside a video, second screen apps and more. You can imagine an infinity of interactions between a stream and others components like texts, images, audio...
